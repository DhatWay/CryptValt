04 — How It Works

FOUR
STEPS

01
Inventor Submits
Idea encrypts in browser with AES-256-GCM. Encrypted blob uploads to IPFS. Only the inventor holds the key.
02
AI Scores Without Seeing
Claude AI analyzes the public teaser and metadata across 14 dimensions to generate a dollar valuation. The encrypted idea is never decrypted.
03
Investors Bid Blind
Sealed blind auction on Ethereum. Investors commit bids without seeing others. Winning bid held in smart contract escrow.
04
Key Delivered, Funds Released
Inventor delivers decryption key. Smart contract releases 80% to inventor automatically. 20% to platform. Royalties on every future resale.
