⬡ Protocol

FIVE
STEPS

Every step is autonomous. No humans. No middlemen. No trust required.

01
Inventor Submits
Drop your idea PDF or fill in the form. Claude AI reads it, auto-fills all fields, runs a patent search against USPTO and Google Patents, and gives a plain English conflict verdict. Then your idea encrypts with AES-256-GCM directly in your browser. The key never leaves your device.
AES-256-GCM · IPFS · CLIENT-SIDE
02
AI Scores Without Seeing
Claude AI analyzes your public teaser, category, problem statement, and market size across 6 dimensions — marketability, scalability, consumer potential, competitive moat, execution feasibility, and revenue clarity. Generates a full investor report and dollar valuation. Your encrypted idea is never decrypted.
CLAUDE AI · 6 DIMENSIONS · DOLLAR VALUE
03
Investors Bid Blind
Sealed blind auction on Ethereum. Investors see the AI report and teaser — but not the idea. Bids are cryptographically sealed. No one knows what anyone else bid. Winning bid held in smart contract escrow automatically.
ETHEREUM · SEALED BIDS · ESCROW
04
Key Delivered, Funds Released
Inventor delivers decryption key on-chain. Smart contract verifies delivery and releases 80% to inventor automatically. 20% to platform. All sales are final — no refunds after key delivery. Zero humans involved at any stage.
80% TO INVENTOR · AUTOMATIC · FINAL
05
NDA + Resale Rights
Upon purchase, investor signs a binding on-chain NDA — legally prohibited from using, copying, or disclosing the idea. Investor may relist the idea for auction at any time. CryptValt takes 20% of the resale. The original inventor earns their royalty automatically on every future transaction in perpetuity.
ON-CHAIN NDA · RESALE RIGHTS · PERPETUAL ROYALTIES
